# Domain Research

Use this skill to produce concise research briefs for product and engineering decisions.

## Research Focus

- Domain terminology and glossary.
- User roles, workflows, and pain points.
- Business processes and edge cases.
- External standards, laws, regulations, and compliance concerns.
- Competitor or reference product behavior.
- Risks, assumptions, and open questions.

## Output

```markdown
## Summary

<Concise findings.>

## Key Terms

- <term>: <meaning>

## User Workflows

- <workflow>

## Constraints

- <constraint>

## Assumptions

- <assumption>

## Open Questions

- <question>

## Sources

- <source or "No external sources used">
```

Separate sourced facts from assumptions.
