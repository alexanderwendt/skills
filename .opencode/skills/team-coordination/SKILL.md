# Team Coordination

Use this skill to coordinate the project-local opencode team.

## Routing

- Product/domain uncertainty: `domain-researcher`.
- Business rules or constraints: `requirements-analyst`.
- Story/specification work: `story-refiner`.
- Technical design: `software-designer` or `architect`.
- Execution breakdown: `task-planner`.
- Implementation: language or frontend engineer.
- Verification: `test-engineer`.
- Final review: `code-reviewer`.

## Handoff

Use `docs/team/handoff-template.md` for non-trivial work. Include goal, scope, context, relevant files/docs, constraints, expected output, and verification expectations.

Keep handoffs small and focused. Do not delegate vague work when one clarifying question would remove ambiguity.
